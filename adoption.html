Adoption of Animals


 Similar to any other zoo, Pilikula has its own adoption procedures to provide a safe and protected environment to the animals. Adoption of animals would help the zoos in involving conscious and esteemed people and companies in bringing more awareness about the plight of the endangered species.


Here in Pilikula, animals can be adopted by donors, who are willing to donate cost of food, maintenance and healthcare for the animals. There are certain benefits of being a donor here, with one of the benefits being unlimited free entry to the donor and their family members throughout the process of adoption.












The names of the donors are also displayed near the enclosures. The maintenance cost may vary, considering the the large variety of animals here. Maintenance of bigger animals like lions and tigers require a great deal of money approximating to 4 lakh rupees per year, whereas smaller beings like birds require as little as 5000 to 10000 rupees per year.
